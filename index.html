About Me

My name is Keenan and I am a 19 year old developer from Minnesota. I am currently a sophomore studying Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin — Madison. I have experience in starting a company and working as a team to develop cool software, websites and more.

I work at JRiver, Inc. during the summer where I develop home automation software. I am also the owner and lead developer of The Avicus Network, a service that has hosted competitive multiplayer games for more than a million users. I am always interested in opportunities such as research, internships, and software or web development positions.

Let's talk — contact me at keenan@keenant.com and check out my résumé.
